HERO IMAGE DIRECTION

The hero image for each Journey page is a strategic storytelling element.

Do NOT use generic business stock photos.

Do NOT use corporate handshake imagery.

Do NOT use isolated portraits.

The image should immediately communicate the aspiration of the journey through authentic human interaction, diversity and shared purpose.

The visual style should be:

• Warm
• Hopeful
• Authentic
• Cooperative
• International
• Community-oriented
• Contemporary
• Natural lighting
• Real-world documentary style

Images should occupy approximately the upper 40–50% of the hero section and integrate naturally with the page headline.

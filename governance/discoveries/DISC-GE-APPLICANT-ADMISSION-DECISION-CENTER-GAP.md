Title

Applicant Admission Decision Center Gap

Repository Truth

The repository already provides admissions decision runtime capabilities, including decision listing for each prospect.

Internal admissions workspaces already consume this runtime.

No applicant-facing admission decision center currently exists.

Applicants cannot view admission decisions, offers, or decision history from the public Growth Engine experience.

Conclusion

The next bounded Repository Reality mutation is an Applicant Admission Decision Center that consumes the existing admissions decision runtime without backend or database mutation.

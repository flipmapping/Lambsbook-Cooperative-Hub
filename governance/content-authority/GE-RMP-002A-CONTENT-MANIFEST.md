Transformation Contract

- preserve semantic fidelity during translation;
- preserve existing JSON schemas;
- preserve existing translation keys;

Execution SHALL NOT:

- invent programme information;
- invent scholarship details;
- invent admissions requirements;
- invent university statistics;
- invent marketing claims.

Every public-facing statement shall be traceable to one or more approved
content authority sources listed above.

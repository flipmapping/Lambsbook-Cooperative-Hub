Business Purpose

Establish the cooperative's canonical capability for recording, governing, and auditing admission decisions throughout the admissions journey.

This capability SHALL provide an immutable decision history for every prospect while preserving complete operational traceability and Release 1 runtime integrity.

Release 1 Scope

The capability SHALL support:

- Admission Decision Recording
- Decision Status Tracking
- Decision History
- Decision Rationale
- Decision Timestamp
- Decision Maker
- Offer Readiness
- Immutable Decision Audit Trail

This Founder Decision authorizes ONLY the business capability.

No repository mutation is authorized by this Founder Decision.

Infrastructure SHALL be authorized separately under INF-010E34A.

Repository mutation SHALL require subsequent RMP authorities.
